Chilling Effect: A Poem by A Chokecherry-Eating Poetry-Man

Doxes here, and lawsuits there,
The internet's dangerous everywhere.
AI tools make it easier,
Swatting's never been breasier.
It's scary on these globe-reaching social nets -
That's a chilling effect.

Worried about mixing personal and work,
All these different socials built by jerks.
Re-configured again, every different way.
Gosh, re-inventing wheels sure let's them harvest pennies!
So easy to fuck up, when they make it this hard -
It's part of the chilling effect.

Grandmas, grandpas, uncles and aunts -
Nobody can keep up with the horrible apps.
Even younger cousins, nieces or nephews can't!
Horrible software, firmware and hardware - plus malware and spyware too.
There's a plethora of ways for them to pinpoint you!

Worried in a private family gathering.
Worried in a small public bar.
Worried when you park and leave your car.
Worried about everyone's personal phones.
Shoddy AI can detect something it misheard,
And lock down a life until the lawyer says it's all cleared.
That's another chilling effect.
So many chilling effects in the news.
Chilling effect, chilling effect.
It's all chilling effects.
