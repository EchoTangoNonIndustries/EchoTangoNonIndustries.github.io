Chilling Effect: A Poem by A Chokecherry-Eating Poetry-Man

Doxes here, and lawsuits there,
Dangers on the internet everywhere.
AI tools make it easier,
Swatting has never been breasier.
You share less on these globe-reaching social nets -
That's a chilling effect.

Worried about mixing work and personal,
All these different socials to be configured, and
Re-configured again, every different way.
Gosh, that sounds like re-inventing wheels!
(You get what you pay for right?)
(Cough cough, with open-source you get what you, me, she, they, them, etc pay for.)
So easy to fuck up, when they make it this hard -
It's part of the chilling effect.

Grandmas, grandpas, uncles and aunts -
Nobody can keep up with the horrible apps.
Even some younger cousins, nieces or nephews can't!
Horrible programs, software, firmware - it's all related
Worried in a private family gathering, or
A small public bar -
I hope someone doesn't take out their phone "for their personal use",
And have some shoddy AI detect something right their in the hardware.
That's another chilling effect.
So many chilling effects in the news.
Chilling effect, chilling effect.
It's all chilling effects.
