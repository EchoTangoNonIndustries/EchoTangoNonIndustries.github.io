A Beacon of Light

Well, then. The world appears to be fucked, and this great pillar seems like an obvious destination. If it's filled with rich assholes and their security guards and murderbots, we fight our way in or die trying. Slowly starving to death out here doesn't seem like a great way to go out. Especially without anyone to talk to, or even just a box of movies and music and porn.
