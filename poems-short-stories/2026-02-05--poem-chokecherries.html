Poem: Chokecherries

Dark purple berries,
The tastiest treat.
Ripe at the end of summer, With just enough sweet.

They dry out your mouth,
But don't taste bitter.
They're sitting there to be picked -
Don't let them fall as litter!

A globule of taste,
But sometimes an invasive weed.
Don't forget in your haste -
Spit out those seeds!
