Short Story: The Others

Squinting and muttering, the boy wakes up. He shovels some vitamins and instant coffee into his mouth, and tries to start his day. Make a checklist on paper - the electronic gadget broke. He's trying to figure out how to repair his truck from old videos. It's a mix of parts, but it still works. Luckily the fuel hasn't run out yet, but he wants to turn it electric next.

His days usually consist of exercise when he's awake. Insomnia's a bitch, so he can't jog as often. He'll regret it later; Right now he hasn't been chased down in the night. Find an old gym and lift some dusty weights. Do some sit-ups and medicine-ball, and get better at chin-ups. Do squats with as heavy a dumbbell as possible - there's some broken shit he needs to haul out of his hidy-hole.
